Tips for Success

  • Use clear labels linked with for/id.
  • Group related inputs with <fieldset> and <legend>.
  • Prefer native validation via required, type, pattern, min/max.
  • Provide helpful placeholders and hints.
  • Ensure keyboard focus styles are visible.
